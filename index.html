WELCOME TO MTN UGANDA

About the Company

MTN Uganda is the largest telecom company in Uganda, with 11.2 million subscribers, accounting for 55 percent market share, as of 30 June 2017. By 31 December 2019, its customer base had increased to 12.6 million customers.

MTN Uganda is one of the largest taxpayers in the country,contributing significantly to the national economy.
